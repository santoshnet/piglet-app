Privacy Policy

📅 Effective: January 1, 2026
🔄 Last Updated: March 2026
📖 ~5 min read
🛡️
Your privacy is our priority. We believe in complete transparency about how your data is handled. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it. We never sell your personal data.
Section 01

Introduction & Who We Are

Piglet ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered expense tracking and personal financial advisor application developed by Piglet App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with full transparency and respect.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Piglet mobile application (iOS & Android) and our associated website at pigletapp.com. By using Piglet, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with a great financial management experience:

Data you provide directly:

  • Account information: name, email address (via Google Sign-In or Email/Password)
  • Financial data you enter: transactions, expense categories, budgets, and savings goals
  • Profile information: profile picture (from Google account if using Google Sign-In)
  • AI chat messages: questions and conversations with the Piglet AI advisor

Data collected automatically:

  • App usage analytics: features used, screens viewed, session duration
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, app version
  • Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability
  • Firebase Analytics data for understanding app performance
  • SMS transaction data (Android only, when enabled): Bank and payment app SMS messages containing transaction information (amounts, dates, merchant names). This data is processed locally and only transaction details are extracted and stored.
💳
We do NOT collect your bank account passwords, credit card numbers, or directly link to your bank accounts. All financial data in Piglet is manually entered by you.
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide, improve, and personalize the Piglet experience:

  • To provide AI-powered financial insights and advice tailored to your spending patterns
  • To sync your data across devices and enable Google Drive backup/restore
  • To send bill reminders and budget alerts via push notifications
  • To analyze aggregate (anonymous) usage patterns to improve app features
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important updates
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse of the service
⚠️
AI Processing: When you use the AI advisor, your questions and relevant expense data are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Please do not share sensitive personal identifiers (PAN, Aadhaar, bank passwords) in chat messages.
Section 04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Primary data storage via Google Firebase — encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3
  • Local data is stored using encrypted AsyncStorage on your device
  • Google Drive backup data is encrypted and stored in your personal Google Drive — only you have access
  • Authentication is handled via Firebase Authentication — we never store passwords in plain text
  • Regular security reviews and automated vulnerability scanning
🔑
Google Drive Backup: When you enable backup, data is stored in your own Google Drive. We cannot access your Google Drive. You can delete backup files at any time directly from your Drive.
Section 05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Limited data sharing occurs only in these specific circumstances:

  • Google Firebase: Hosting, authentication, and database services
  • Google Gemini API: Processing AI advisor queries (only query text + your summarized expense context is sent)
  • Google Drive API: Backup and restore functionality (requires your explicit authorization)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority

All third-party service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements that prohibit them from using your data for unauthorized purposes.

Section 06

App Permissions

Piglet may request the following device permissions. We only ask for what's necessary:

  • Notifications: To send bill reminders and budget alerts. You can disable at any time in device settings.
  • Internet Access: Required for AI features, sync, and cloud backup.
  • Google Account Access: For Google Sign-In and optional Google Drive backup.
  • Storage / Camera (optional): If you scan receipts or bills. Not required for core functionality.
  • SMS Access (Android only, optional): To automatically detect bank transactions from SMS messages. Only reads messages from banks and payment apps (HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Paytm, PhonePe, GPay, etc.). This feature is completely optional and only requested when you choose to enable automatic transaction import.
📱
SMS Transaction Detection: When enabled, Piglet reads SMS messages from banks and payment providers to automatically detect financial transactions. We only process messages that contain transaction information (amounts, dates, account details). Your SMS data is processed locally on your device and is never shared with third parties. You can disable this feature at any time or choose to add transactions manually instead.
Section 07

Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data. You can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account within the app
  • Export: Export your expense data in CSV or PDF format from the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data permanently from app Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection in app Settings → Privacy
  • Portability: Your data can be exported before account deletion

Account deletion permanently removes all your data from our servers within 30 days of the request. Google Drive backup files must be deleted manually from your Drive.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Piglet is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Notify you via a push notification in the Piglet app
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • For material changes, request re-consent if required by law

Continued use of Piglet after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
